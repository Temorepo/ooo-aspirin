Packagecom.threerings.ui
Classpublic class CustomButton
InheritanceCustomButton flash.display.Sprite

A Sprite that simulates SimpleButton behavior and gets around its annoying limitations. If a SimpleButton is customized with MovieClip-based states, those MovieClips will be restarted every time the SimpleButton's state changes. CustomButton doesn't do this.
